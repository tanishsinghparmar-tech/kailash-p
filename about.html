A Legacy Of Purity Since 1972

Our journey began in 1972 with a simple mission: To provide families with fresh, pure and trustworthy flour.

For over five decades, households across Kota have trusted us for quality, consistency, and nutrition. Every grain is carefully selected. Every batch is freshly ground. Every customer is treated like family.

Today we combine tradition with convenience through home delivery and online ordering, ensuring the warmth of fresh rotis continues to bring families together.
