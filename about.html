Our Story

From a small chakki in Kota to a trusted household name.

Founded in 1972 in Raza Nagar, Kota, Kailash Pavitra Atta began with a simple mission: to provide families with the purest, freshly ground wheat flour.

Over the decades, we have maintained our commitment to traditional stone-grinding methods, ensuring that the natural fibers, nutrients, and the authentic taste of wheat are preserved in every grain.

"We don't just sell flour; we deliver health and trust to your family's dining table."
